About Todd & Gary

Meet Todd (from Adelaide) and Gary (from Sydney)—two Australian drummers who followed their passion all the way to Berlin. Each has a strong foundation in music and performance, having studied at universities in Australia where they honed not just their technique, but also their collaborative skills through band leadership and teaching. Their shared vision? To use rhythm as a universal language for bringing people together.

From Street Corners to Corporate Workshops

Their journey began on a lively Berlin street, each armed with only a few buckets and an unwavering love for percussion. A spontaneous jam session revealed a powerful synergy—not just in their drumming, but also in their desire to inspire. Over time, they discovered that the same principles powering their busking success—trust, teamwork, creativity, and the courage to be heard—could transform corporate environments, community groups, and beyond.

Why Bucket Drumming?

Bucket drumming is fun, accessible, and unexpectedly impactful. For Todd and Gary, it’s the perfect medium to help participants:

  • Embrace inclusivity: No fancy instruments or prior musical experience required—just an open mind and willingness to collaborate.
  • Synchronize and communicate: Keeping a unified beat encourages active listening, trust, and clear communication, key elements for any thriving team.
  • Build self-confidence and face fears: Overcoming stage fright or performance anxiety leads to real personal and professional growth.

Whether performing on a street corner or leading an energetic workshop, Todd and Gary see each bucket as a canvas for creativity, and each performance as a chance to ignite enthusiasm, connection, and meaningful interaction.

The Heart of Their Workshops

Today, Todd and Gary channel their passion for performance and education into Bucket Drumming Workshops that unlock creativity, team spirit, and personal breakthroughs. As remote work environments become more common, they believe it’s more crucial than ever to provide hands-on experiences where colleagues can reconnect, rediscover a sense of community, and celebrate shared accomplishments.

After a session with Todd and Gary, participants often leave with:

  • Energized motivation for everyday work and challenges.
  • Increased self-confidence from stepping outside their comfort zone.
  • Enhanced communication skills—essential for staying connected and aligned in modern hybrid or remote team settings.
  • Stronger bonds with fellow drummers, colleagues, or friends, built on a steady collective beat.

Join Todd and Gary on this rhythmic journey and see how the power of a few buckets can revitalize your team, spark innovation, and help everyone drum up the confidence they need to thrive—both on stage and in the workplace.
