About Todd and Gary

Todd and Gary, both Australian buskers residing in Berlin, have been passionately playing bucket drums on the streets for over a decade. Their journey began with a shared love for rhythm and a desire to bring joy to passersby. Over the years, they've discovered that bucket drumming is not only a source of personal joy but also a powerful tool for fostering teamwork and collaboration.

Through their workshops, Todd and Gary leverage their extensive experience to teach businesses how to harness the strengths of their teams while addressing weaknesses in a fun and engaging environment. Their unique approach ensures that participants not only learn the art of bucket drumming but also gain valuable insights into effective teamwork.
