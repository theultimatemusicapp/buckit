About Todd & Gary

Meet Todd (from Adelaide) and Gary (from Sydney)—two Australian drummers who followed their passion all the way to Berlin. They first crossed paths by chance on a bustling Berlin street, each with little more than a few buckets and an unbreakable love for rhythm. A spontaneous jam session soon revealed they shared not only musical chemistry, but a common dream: to bring people together through simple, powerful beats.

From Street Corners to Corporate Workshops

When they began busking, Todd and Gary discovered that bucket drumming was more than street entertainment—it was a way to captivate crowds, build confidence, and communicate without words. Over time, they realized these same principles of trust, teamwork, and creativity could transform corporate and community environments as well.

Why Bucket Drumming?

Bucket drumming is fun, accessible, and surprisingly impactful. Todd and Gary believe that:

  • Anyone can join in: No fancy equipment or training needed—just an open mind.
  • Rhythm unites teams: Synchronizing beats builds trust, harmony, and collaboration.
  • Confidence grows with practice: Overcoming stage fright and self-doubt leads to real personal and professional growth.

Whether performing in front of a café or running a high-energy workshop, Todd and Gary see each bucket as a canvas for creativity, and every performance as a chance to inspire.

The Heart of Their Workshops

Today, Todd and Gary channel their passion into Bucket Drumming Workshops that spark creativity, build genuine connections, and inspire personal breakthroughs. Participants leave with:

  • Fresh energy and motivation to tackle everyday challenges.
  • Newfound confidence from stepping out of comfort zones.
  • Stronger bonds with colleagues, friends, or fellow drummers.

Join them on this rhythmic journey and experience firsthand how a pair of humble buckets can ignite team spirit, boost communication, and drive personal growth—both on and off the stage.
