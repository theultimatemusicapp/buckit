90-Minute Workshop Itinerary

Here’s a sample breakdown of our core 90-minute Bucket Drumming Workshop, which can be adapted to your team’s specific goals and needs.

  1. 0–10 Minutes: Welcome & Warm-Up
    A friendly introduction to the facilitators, the buckets, and basic rhythmic exercises to get everyone comfortable.
  2. 10–30 Minutes: Foundational Rhythms
    Learn and practice simple rhythmic patterns. These form the building blocks for more advanced group drumming.
  3. 30–50 Minutes: Collaborative Drumming
    Split into smaller teams to develop unique beats and experiment with layering different rhythms together.
  4. 50–60 Minutes: Crowd Interaction & Overcoming Stage Fear
    Dive into effective ways to engage an audience, conquer the fear of public speaking, and present with confidence. We’ll discuss body language, vocal projection, and the importance of reading the crowd.
  5. 60–70 Minutes: Performance Practice
    Come together for a collective performance focusing on coordination, timing, and clear communication—while applying crowd-engagement techniques.
  6. 70–90 Minutes: Reflection & Q&A
    Discuss key insights, successes, and areas of growth. Participants share feedback, ask questions, and solidify the lessons learned.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do we need any prior drumming or musical experience?

Not at all! Our workshops are designed for beginners and seasoned musicians alike. We’ll guide you from the basics onward.

2. What equipment is required?

We provide all necessary drumming equipment, including buckets and sticks. Just bring a positive attitude and willingness to learn.

3. Can you accommodate remote or hybrid work environments?

Absolutely. We have adapted sections of our workshop for online participation. Contact us for virtual or hybrid options.

4. How do I prepare my team for the workshop?

Simply encourage them to wear comfortable clothing and be ready to move a bit. We’ll take care of the rest!

5. Are you able to travel outside of Berlin?

Yes, we often travel for corporate events. Additional travel fees may apply depending on distance and logistics.
