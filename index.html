About Us

Gary and Todd are professional drummers, band leaders, and drum teachers with formal training from Australian universities. Their expertise in performance and music education underpins an exciting, hands-on approach to strengthening teamwork in corporate environments.

They understand how critical it is for teams to work together in sync—much like drummers must keep a steady rhythm to support a band. This workshop teaches participants to listen carefully, embrace the courage to be heard, and coordinate efforts so everyone stays on beat.

Workshop Insights

In a world where remote work has become the norm, many teams are struggling with disconnection and lack of motivation. Our Bucket Drumming Workshop addresses these challenges head-on, fostering real human interaction and energizing the entire office. Here are some workshop highlights:

From Struggle to Stage

Discover how drumming on buckets helped Todd and Gary overcome hardships, ignite creativity, and inspire audiences across Berlin.

Listening & Bravery

Learn the power of truly listening to those around you and building confidence to be boldly heard—so the team stays united in purpose.

Timing & Synchronization

Realize how getting “in sync” is critical to your team's success. By aligning your rhythms, you amplify the entire group's performance.

Capturing & Engaging an Audience

Uncover simple techniques to command attention immediately—through dynamic rhythms, confident body language, and a welcoming group vibe.

Communicating with Confidence

Master the art of reading body language, varying your tone, and sharing ideas passionately—skills that stand out in every meeting or pitch.

Teamwork & Crowd Interaction

Find out why trust and communication are the backbeat of any team. Synchronization doesn’t just apply to music—it applies to business, too.

Building Self-Confidence & Personal Growth

Step outside your comfort zone, savor small successes, and witness how these efforts compound to drive both individual and team success.

Post-COVID Reconnection

Many companies now rely on home office setups, eroding the sense of community and shared identity. Our workshop revitalizes those bonds, sparking creativity, motivation, and a renewed sense of shared purpose.

Why It Works: Bucket drumming offers an energetic, interactive method for rekindling team spirit, effective communication, and overall confidence in the workplace.
