Frequently Asked Questions

How long are the drum lessons?
The duration of drum lessons varies based on your chosen program. Typically, lessons range from 30 minutes to 1 hour per session.
What equipment do I need for drum lessons?
For in-person lessons, you'll need a drum kit. For online lessons, a basic drum pad or electronic drum kit will suffice.
Can I switch programs if I want to advance or change my focus?
Yes, DrumMaster allows you to switch programs as you advance or if you wish to focus on a different drumming style.
What if I miss a lesson?
If you miss a lesson, you can easily reschedule it through your DrumMaster dashboard. We aim to be flexible with your scheduling needs.
Do I need prior drumming experience to join?
No prior experience is required. DrumMaster offers lessons for all skill levels, from complete beginners to advanced drummers.
